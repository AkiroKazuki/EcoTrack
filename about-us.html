EcoTrack is an online platform that allows anyone to monitor real-time environmental changes. With EcoTrack, you can participate in nature conservation efforts by tracking the reforestation process, monitoring the rate of deforestation, and measuring water quality in various areas. Accurate and up-to-date data will help you understand the impact human activity has on the environment and take real action to protect our planet.

With this platform you can also understand more about nature and why nature is important to protect.
